The Model

Great upsell is signal-driven and pre-qualified. A trigger fires, the account is checked against a set of prerequisites, and only qualified opportunities flow into a repeatable execution play. The system continuously watches for signals rather than relying on reps to manually hunt.

4 · Future-State Statements

In 2–3 years, what world-class looks like for this motion.

The model will continuously monitor regulatory, product, and account signals to automatically surface upsell opportunities — no manual hunting.
The model will auto-qualify each opportunity against health, usage, coverage, fit, and delivery-readiness prerequisites before a seller ever engages.
The model will rank and prioritize opportunities across the book when multiple accounts qualify, so effort goes to the highest-value plays first.
The model will auto-generate the sales play — targeted messaging, indicative pricing, presentation, and script — tailored to persona and account context.
Clients will experience timely, relevant outreach that anticipates needs created by regulatory and ecosystem change (e.g., Basel) rather than generic product pitches.
Sellers will experience a guided, repeatable play from trigger to signing, with follow-up and nurturing driven by captured meeting insights.
Nasdaq will convert existing whitespace into expansion revenue faster and more predictably, with full traceability from signal to closed deal.
